Project Idea

iwore is a social media application designed to bring fashionistas of the world together. Explore fashion through the inspiration of others by sharing your outfit's intimate details, including; cost, size, jewellery, makeup and the store from which the user brought all of the items. This app removes the stresses of getting ready and answering questions like what is appropriate to wear at that venue/event and what they are wearing. Users can connect by tagging into locations and updating images of their outfits with the attached metadata. In turn, this information becomes searchable to other users looking into that venue. Additionally, users can upload the same data to a closed event. Here, users can show the outfits they plan to wear to an upcoming social outing with friends, such as a wedding. Following the application's success, this could extend to a get ready with me feature that lets budding makeup artists and hairstylists present tutorials of how their styles came alive.
The motivation behind iwore will be to promote a positive environment in which women and men alike can share their fashion and remove the stresses of getting ready. This application aims in promoting smaller businesses by pushing data tailored around the user's location, heightening the visibility of an otherwise smothered industry. iwore will be proud supporters of body confidence removing the high quality, edited model shots we are so often exposed to and replacing them with the real you.
